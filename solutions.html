Our Solutions

Structured engagements designed around your most important decisions.

We don’t sell retainers. We sell outcomes. Every engagement has a clear deliverable and a defined end state.

01
Rapid Assessment
🕑 2–3 weeks

Decision Clarity Sprint

Identify your 3–5 highest-stakes decisions and the uncertainty driving them. Walk away knowing exactly what you need to decide, who owns each call, and what information would change your answer.

Prioritized decision map with owners and timelines
Assumption registry for each critical decision
Signal monitoring framework (what to watch and why)
90-minute leadership alignment session
Best for: Leadership teams who feel overwhelmed by competing priorities and need rapid clarity on where to focus.
02
Market Intelligence
🕑 4–6 weeks

Strategic Market Assessment

Deep analysis of your competitive landscape, customer dynamics, and market trajectory. Separates genuine disruption from noise and translates findings into decision-ready intelligence.

Competitive landscape analysis with strategic implications
Customer segment dynamics and shifting needs assessment
Market signal classification (act now / monitor / ignore)
Strategic options framework with risk-adjusted scenarios
Board-ready summary with executive recommendations
Best for: Companies facing a major strategic choice (M&A, market entry, product pivot, pricing strategy) who need intelligence before committing capital.
03
Execution Alignment
🕑 6–10 weeks

FutureReady Operating System

Build the decision infrastructure your organization needs to stay aligned as conditions evolve. Establishes the rhythms, triggers, and accountability structures that prevent strategic drift.

Decision governance framework with ownership model
Strategic assumption tracking system
Early warning trigger playbook
Cross-functional alignment workshops (2–3 sessions)
90-day implementation roadmap with milestones
30-day check-in after implementation
Best for: Organizations that keep making the right decisions on paper but struggle to maintain alignment through execution.
04
Ongoing Advisory
🕑 Quarterly

Fractional Strategy Partner

Ongoing strategic partnership for leadership teams that want experienced outside perspective on a consistent basis. We become your thinking partner across the decisions that matter most.

Monthly strategy sessions with Robert and/or Jack
Quarterly signal review and priority recalibration
On-call access for high-stakes decisions as they arise
Annual strategic planning facilitation
Best for: CEOs and leadership teams who want a trusted outside perspective without the cost of a full-time chief strategy officer.
How We Work

Principles that guide every engagement.

🎯

Outcome-first

We define the decision you need to make before we define the work. Every engagement is scoped around a specific outcome, not a set of deliverables.

⏰

Speed over perfection

We work fast. Clarity at 80% confidence beats certainty at 6 months. We get you to actionable decisions quickly, then refine as you learn.

🤝

Direct and candid

We tell you what we actually think. If we see a gap between your strategy and your execution, we say so. You don’t need a consultant to agree with you.

Our Promise

30-day clarity guarantee.

If you complete a Decision Clarity Sprint and don’t feel you have materially more clarity on your most important decisions, we’ll work with you until you do. No exceptions, no conditions.
