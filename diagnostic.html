FutureReady Diagnostic

How ready is your organization to act on what’s changing?

This 10-minute assessment identifies the specific gaps in your decision-making infrastructure — and gives you a clear starting point for building FutureReady capability.

What This Is

A diagnostic built for leaders, not consultants.

Most assessments are designed to sell you something. This one is designed to tell you something true about how your organization makes decisions — even if the answer is uncomfortable.

What you’ll get

A clear picture of where your decision-making infrastructure is strong, where it has dangerous gaps, and which gaps to address first. Takes 10 minutes. Results are immediate.

Who it’s for

CEOs, COOs, CSOs, and leadership teams who want to move faster and with more confidence — and need to know what’s actually slowing them down.

The Questions

Answer honestly. Pressure-test your instincts.

Rate each statement 1–5 (1 = not at all true, 5 = absolutely true for our organization).

A
Decision Clarity

We could name our top 3 strategic priorities and they would be the same ones we named 90 days ago.
Every major initiative has one named person accountable for the decision, not "leadership" or a committee.
We rarely revisit decisions that were already made — unless new evidence genuinely warrants it.

B
Market Intelligence

We can distinguish which market signals require action from those that just require monitoring.
We know what our top 3 competitors are doing and how it affects our strategy right now.
Our customer intelligence is based on structured data, not anecdotes from the sales team.

C
Execution Alignment

If you asked each member of our leadership team to list our top 3 priorities, they would give the same answer.
Our teams spend more time executing than debating what we should be working on.
When conditions change, our team adapts execution without requiring a full strategy re-plan.

D
Adaptive Capacity

We have pre-agreed triggers: specific conditions that would cause us to revisit our most important decisions.
The assumptions behind our biggest bets are written down and reviewed quarterly.

Reading Your Score

What your results mean.

10–25: At Risk

Significant gaps in decision infrastructure. Strategy drift is likely already costing you. Prioritize Decision Clarity Sprint immediately.

26–40: Developing

Some strong foundations but critical gaps remain. You’re moving, but losing energy to misalignment. Focus on the 2–3 lowest-scoring areas first.

41–55: FutureReady

You have strong decision infrastructure. The opportunity now is to formalize and systematize what’s working — so it survives leadership changes and growth.
